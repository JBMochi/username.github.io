The Kaha Engine

How it works

Five stages. Fully automated. You focus on the floor — Kaha fills your calendar.

01
Ingest
Your contact list is cleaned, deduplicated, and imported. CSVs, Mindbody exports, spreadsheets — all accepted.
02
Segment
AI scores every contact 1–10 for reactivation likelihood. Three segments: lapsed member, warm lead, cold inquiry.
03
Sequence
5 personalised emails generated per contact. Written in your gym's voice. Sounds like you — not a marketing agency.
04
Activate
Emails drip automatically over 14 days. Replies are detected and scored by AI. Hot leads flagged instantly.
05
Convert
You receive a weekly hot lead list with talking points. Make the call. Close the member. Kaha handles everything else.
